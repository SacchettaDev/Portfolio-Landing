About Me

Hello, my name is Alexandre Sacchetta Virginio. I’m 23 years old, and this is how my story with code began.

Ever since I finished high school, I’ve been fascinated by two worlds: programming and cybersecurity — building things, understanding how systems work, and learning how to protect them. That curiosity pushed me to start a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering in Brazil, where I spent my days studying and my nights chasing real skills.

Not long after, I earned my first opportunity as a Back-end Developer, working mainly with Python. Suddenly, what I was learning in class had a real purpose — solving problems, writing clean code, and seeing my work actually help people.

After about a year balancing college and work, life surprised me in the best way: I met my wife. That moment changed everything. I moved to Canada, got married, and decided to restart with a bigger goal — building a strong career here.

In 2025, I began my Computer Programming diploma at Algonquin College in Ottawa. Now I’m looking for my first opportunity as a developer in Canada — a team where I can contribute with discipline, communication, and real back-end fundamentals, while growing into a professional software developer with a strong cybersecurity mindset.
